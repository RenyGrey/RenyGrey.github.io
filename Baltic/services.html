Frequent questions

Delivery and shipping

  • What country do you ship to?

    • We ship all over the world, except for Russia, Belarus, Pakistan and Turkey.

  • What are the delivery methods and times?

    • We use the DHL Express service for deliveries in France and abroad. All our shipments are made
    with delivery against signature to avoid thefts in the mailboxes.

    If you are not present on the day of delivery, you will be able to choose different delivery options
    on the DHL.com website, such as delivery at a later date, a drop-off
    at a relay point, a drop-off in a safe place, or the possibility of giving the package to a third party.
    Deliveries take 3 to 5 days.

  • Is delivery free?

    • International delivery is offered from 80€ of purchase. For a product in stock, the delivery time
    varies between 3 to 5 days.

  • Do I have to pay duties and taxes upon delivery of my order?

    • In France and in the European Union, taxes and customs fees are included in the price of
    your order.
    Outside the European Union, taxes and customs fees are not included (except for the United
    Kingdom for which we collect the VAT below 150€ of purchase). You may be subject to local
    duty/import taxes, if they apply. These taxes will be claimed on behalf of local customs by our
    DHL carrier prior to delivery. Unfortunately, we are unable to advise you of the amount of these
    charges in advance and advise you to contact the duty services in your country to obtain an
    estimated duty amount and the base rate of VAT. If the taxes and customs fees are not paid, your
    order will be returned to us.

  • How can i track my package?

    • To track your package, go to our order tracking page and enter your order number and email address.

  • What if I want to change my delivery address?

    • If you wish to modify the delivery address you provided when you placed your order, contact us directly from our contact page or by phone at +33 1 40 16 07 17.

  • What should i Do if i have a delivery problem?

    • For any delivery problem with your order, please contact us directly from our contact page or by phone at +33 1 40 16 07 17. We will make the necessary arrangements with the carrier and will do everything possible to ensure that your package arrives correctly at its destination.
