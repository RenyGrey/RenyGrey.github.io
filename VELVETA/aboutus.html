Our History

In the heart of our city, where laughter echoes through cobbled streets and aromas of garlic and basil float through the air, Velveta was born. Inspired by generations of Italian family kitchens, Velveta is more than a restaurant — it’s a story of passion, perseverance, and pasta. Our founder grew up savoring Sunday dinners in a small Tuscan village, where meals were a sacred ritual, not just sustenance. With every lovingly prepared dish, Velveta brings those cherished memories to life — from Nonna’s silky handmade fettuccine to wood-fired pizzas that sing of Napoli’s spirit.

Velveta Is Born

Velveta isn’t just about food; it’s about connection. It’s the clinking of wine glasses, the joy of shared laughter, and the comfort of being known — all wrapped in the warm glow of Italian hospitality. Every ingredient is selected with care, every sauce simmered with love, and every guest welcomed like famiglia.Whether it’s your first visit or your fiftieth, Velveta is your table in Italy — right here at home.
