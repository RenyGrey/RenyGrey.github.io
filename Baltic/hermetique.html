An ultra legible
smoked dial

The dial of the Hermétique Tourer Bronze embodies the essence of our toolwatch design, combining timeless elegance with functionality. Every detail has been carefully thought out to make it easy to read time, even in the dark. The dial features a brushed black railway and a slightly smoked central sector, separated by two polished bronze blades. The hour appliques are made of Super-Luminova® C3 X1 and overlap the two sectors in places, offering aplay of depths to the composition as a whole. The "syringe" hands are in polished bronze and fitted with the same Super-Luminova®.

A SINGULAR MATERIAL

Used for its resistance to corrosion, bronze has the property of evolving with the contact of those who handle it. The case and bezel are made of a resistant copper and aluminum alloy (CuAl8) that will develop a patina over time, making each Hermétique Tourer Bronze a truly unique watch.

Durable and confortable
Rubber FKM straps

vulcanized rubber (FKM), an ultra-soft material offering unrivalled
comfort.
